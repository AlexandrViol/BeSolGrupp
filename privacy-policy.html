Privacy Policy

BeSol Grupp is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. The following privacy statement (further ‘Privacy Policy’) is designed to help you understand all the ways we apply to collect and use the information you provide us with and to assist you in making informed decisions when using our site, products, and services. Personal data of our customers and visitors are processed and used with special care.

Collecting Information

While visiting our website or using our products or services you may provide us with your personal data that you consciously disclose. Also, our website uses information collected on an aggregate basis, as you and other visitors browse our website.

We may collect, store and use the following kinds of personal data:

  • Information you provide to us by filling in forms on our website (e.g. by requesting the information about our products or services);
  • Information you provide to us for the purpose of subscribing to our email notifications and/or newsletters;
  • Information you provide to us when submitting your CV (including your contact information);
  • Information contained in or relating to any communication that you send to us or send through this website (including correspondence with us, communication content and metadata associated with the communication);
  • Information that is required to obtain trial or commercial license of our products or otherwise become a registered user of our products or services;
  • Any other personal data that you choose to send to us (i.e. the personal data of a candidate to work who you want to refer);
  • Information about your visits to and use of this website (including your IP address, geographical location, browser type and version, operating system, referral source, length of visit, page views and website navigation paths).

Use of Information Collected

Personal data collected by BeSol Grupp may be used by BeSol Grupp for a wide range of purposes:

  • To inform you of new products and services we will be providing,
  • Communication about past, current, or future projects,
  • Feedback and/or editorial purposes,
  • Marketing and market research,
  • Promotion,
  • Recruitment,
  • Advertising,
  • License generation,
  • Payment processing,
  • Customer support,
  • Statistical analysis,
  • To record your consent to BeSol Grupp to obtain and store your personal data.

For the above-mentioned purposes, we use only personal data that we receive from you through contact forms on our website. Please notice, that we require your explicit consent to this Privacy Policy when you submit information through any of our contact forms or during the process of requesting or purchasing licenses for our products.

Personalizing and identifying information, such as names, postal and email addresses, phone numbers, and other individual information voluntarily provided by visitors to BeSol Grupp may be added to its databases and used for future telephone calls and correspondence.

If you choose to correspond with us through email, we may retain the content of your email messages together with your email address and our responses.

The information collected through third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics and Yandex Metrics can be used to carry out statistical analysis of customer behavior, for software development, content improvement, or customization.

Cookies

Like many other commercial websites, BeSol Grupp website utilizes a standard technology called “cookies” and web server logs to collect information about how our website is used. Data collected through cookies and web server logs may include the date and time of visits, the pages viewed, time spent at our website, and any websites visited just before and just after visiting our own website.

You can opt-out of cookies in your browser anytime, however, please note that you may not be able to take full advantage of our website if you disable them.

Third Party Analytics Software

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses “cookies”, which are text files stored on your computer to enable analysis of website usage by you. The information about your website usage generated by the cookie (including your IP address) will be transferred to a Google server in the U.S. and stored there. Google uses this information to evaluate your use of the website, to prepare reports on website activity, and to provide other services associated with website activity and internet usage to website operators. Google may also share this information with third parties if required by law, or if such third parties process the data on Google’s behalf. The IP address that your browser conveys within the scope of Google Analytics will not be associated with any other data held by Google. You can alter the configuration of your browser to refuse cookies, however, please note that you may not be able to take full advantage of our website if you disable them. By using this website, you agree to the processing of data about you by Google in the previously described manner and for the above-mentioned purpose.

This Website uses Yandex Metrics which is a web tracking tool of Yandex. Yandex Metrics uses so-called cookies. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer and allows an analysis of your use of the website. The information on your use of the Website as generated by the cookie will usually be transferred to a server of Yandex in Russia and stored on such server. Yandex will, as an agent of the person or entity operating this Website, use such information for purposes of analyzing your use of the website in order to create reports on the website activities and in order to render further services related to the use of the website or of the internet to the person or entity operating the Website. Check Yandex’s Terms of Use for more details: https://yandex.com/legal/metrica_termsofuse/ You can prohibit the storage of cookies by way of a respective configuration of your browser software; we inform you, however, that in this case, you may not be able to fully use all functions of this website.

Anonymous data collected through this website

In addition to the information which we collect as described above, we use technology to collect anonymous information about the use of our website. For example, our web server automatically logs which pages of our website our visitors view, their IP addresses, and which web browsers our visitors use. This technology does not identify you personally, it simply enables us to compile statistics about our visitors and their use of our website.

Our website contains hyperlinks to other pages on our website. We may use technology to track how often these links are used and which pages our visitors choose to view on our website. Again this technology does not identify you personally – it simply enables us to compile statistics about the use of these hyperlinks.

Your rights

The European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation and privacy laws in other countries provide certain rights for data subjects.

You have a right to access and rectify the record of your personal data maintained by BeSol Grupp. You are allowed to revoke your consent and request that the BeSol Grupp erase your personal data or cease processing it, subject to certain exceptions.
